Refund & Return Policy - Fusion Mart

At Fusion Mart, customer satisfaction is our priority. We understand that sometimes a product may not meet your expectations, and we are here to help. Our refund and return policy is crafted to ensure a smooth and trustworthy experience for every shopper.

Return Period

We accept returns within 15 days from the date of delivery. If 15 days have passed since your purchase, unfortunately, we can't offer a refund or exchange.

Return Eligibility

To qualify for a return:

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition you received it.
  • It must be in the original packaging, with all tags, manuals, and accessories intact.
  • A proof of purchase (like an order ID or invoice) must be provided.

Items returned that do not meet these criteria may be rejected or refunded at partial value.

Non-Returnable Items

Some items cannot be returned for hygiene, safety, or legal reasons. These include:

  • Perishable goods (e.g., food, flowers)
  • Personal care and hygiene items
  • Gift cards or vouchers
  • Downloadable software or digital products
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"

Exchanges

If your item is defective, damaged, or you received the wrong product, we will gladly exchange it. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with clear photographs of the product and packaging.

Size or variant issues? You'll need to return the item first and place a new order with the correct details.

Refunds

Once your returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed depending on your original payment method:

  • UPI/Online Payment: Refunded directly to the UPI or bank account within 24-48 hours.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Refunded as a credit to your Fusion Mart Wallet, which you can use for future purchases.

Note: Shipping costs are non-refundable, unless the return is due to our error.

How to Start a Return

To initiate a return:

1. Log into your account and go to your Order History.
2. Select the item and click "Return".
3. Follow the instructions provided.
4. Use a trackable courier service to send the item back to the return address.
We do not guarantee receipt of returned items without tracking.
